The columns are the:

  • index of network in the list
  • image of a visualization from the network input
  • image of a visualization from the network output
  • name of the network
  • date that the model was last saved
  • epoch it was saved after
  • delta time in the future being predicted
  • loss that it achieved

A/H 2 A/H: AIA/HMI 2 AIA/HMI

The a/h 2 a/h models convert AIA/HMI data to AIA/HMI frequencies. The input is the 9 channels on the left (or 3 Helioseismic and Magnetic Images) and the output is the 9 channels on the right (or HMI). These are either ground truth or the output of a network, respectively. The metrics are computed across the entire validation set. The A/H specific columns are:

  • average of per pixel relative difference, (y-y_hat) / y
  • average of per pixel absolute difference, y-y_hat
  • percent of pixels within 10% of ground truth
  • percent of pixels within 20% of ground truth
  • percent of pixels within 50% of ground truth

S2F: Stats 2 Flare

The s2f models are all trained on converting statistics from AIA and HMI data to GOES
flare predictions. The input to each model is the following statistics from all 12 HMI and AIA channels:
mean, std, sum, absolute value sum, min, max, median, mode, and range. The SF specific columns are:

  • mAP of the classifier
  • accuracy of the classifier
  • Heidke Skill Score of classifier
  • F1 score of the classififer

The AH pages are sorted by the best pixels within 10% of the truth measure, with intra (but NOT inter)
page sorting possible. The SF pages are sorted by the accuracy of the classifier. Click on a table header to sort by that measure. Alternatively, the columns are from the classification task and represent a number of relevant metrics.
